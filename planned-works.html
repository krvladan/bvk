Planned works

17.06.2022 Planned works on water supplying network at Vračar, Zvezdara and Barajevo municipalities

Due to planned works on water supplying network at Vračar and Zvezdara municipalities, on Friday 17.06.2022, from 8.00 am until 6.00 pm, the consumers in the following streets will have not water supply:

  • Gročanska,
  • Grčića Milenka (between Južni Boulevard and Vojislava Ilića),
  • Južni Boulevard (between Šumatovačka and Grčića Milenka),
  • Vojvode Tankosića,
  • Cerska (between Grčića Milenka and Đerdapska),
  • Pop Stojanova and
  • Vidovdanska.

Due to planned works on water supplying network at Barajevo Municipality, on Friday 17.06.2022, from 9.00 am until 2.00 pm, the consumers in the following streets and residential areas will have not water supply:

  • Nenadovac,
  • Glumčevo brdo,
  • Stara Lipovica,
  • Karaula,
  • Dražanovac,
  • 29. novembra,
  • Lipovički put,
  • Visoka,
  • Branislava Jovanovića,
  • Timočki put,
  • Prvomajska and
  • Brđanska.

18.06.2022 Planned works on water supplying network at Zemun Municipality

Due to planned works on water supplying network at Zemun Municipality, on Saturday 18.06.2022, from 9.00 am until 12.00 am, the consumers in the following streets will have not water supply:

  • Kej oslobođenja (between Masarikov trg and Zmaj Jovina),
  • Masarikov trg and
  • Gospodska (between Masarikov trg and Kej oslobođenja).
Info

Due to unexpected circumstances during performing of planned works, it is possible that, besides named streets, surrounding streets experience low pressure or interruption in water supplying.
